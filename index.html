Tempered Glass

Tempered glass is a type of safety glass processed by controlled thermal or chemical treatments to increase its strength compared with normal glass.

Features:

  • 4-5 times stronger than regular glass
  • Breaks into small granular chunks instead of sharp shards
  • Heat resistant up to 250°C
  • Ideal for shower doors, glass doors, tabletops, and windows

Specifications:

  • Thickness: 4mm, 5mm, 6mm, 8mm, 10mm, 12mm
  • Max Size: 2440mm x 5500mm
  • Price Range: ₹150 - ₹300/sq.ft

Laminated Glass

Laminated glass is made of two or more layers of glass with one or more layers of a protective interlayer bonded between them under heat and pressure.

Features:

  • Enhanced security and safety
  • UV protection (blocks up to 99% of UV rays)
  • Sound reduction properties
  • Ideal for storefronts, skylights, and hurricane-prone areas

Specifications:

  • Thickness: 6.38mm, 8.38mm, 10.38mm, 12.38mm
  • Interlayer Options: Clear, Tinted, Patterned
  • Price Range: ₹200 - ₹400/sq.ft

Frosted Glass

Frosted glass is produced by acid etching or sandblasting clear sheet glass to create a pitted surface that scatters light passing through it.

Features:

  • Provides privacy while allowing light transmission
  • Available in various patterns and opacity levels
  • Easy to clean and maintain
  • Ideal for bathroom windows, office partitions, and decorative panels

Specifications:

  • Thickness: 3mm, 4mm, 5mm, 6mm, 8mm
  • Pattern Options: Rain, Mist, Snow, Custom designs
  • Price Range: ₹180 - ₹350/sq.ft

Mirrors

Our premium mirrors are made with high-quality float glass with a silver backing, providing crystal clear reflection and durability.

Features:

  • Clear, distortion-free reflection
  • Various edge finishing options (polished, beveled, framed)
  • Custom shapes and sizes available
  • Ideal for bathrooms, dressing rooms, and decorative purposes

Specifications:

  • Thickness: 3mm, 4mm, 5mm, 6mm
  • Max Size: 2440mm x 3660mm
  • Price Range: ₹120 - ₹250/sq.ft
